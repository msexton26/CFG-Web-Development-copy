My work

Teaching

I teach at a secondary school and sixth form in West Suffolk, near Bury St. Edmunds. I have been teaching Computer Science for nearly 10 years. Eight of those at my current school. I try to develop a curriculum which offers all students (including girls) the chance to enjoy coding and computing.

One way I try to inspire students to take up computing is through trips to computing locations. My favourite being San Francisco where we take students to visit people I know at Google.

Volunteering

Digital Schoolhouse

This is an organisation which helps to support teachers in primary schools to teach computing. This is through leading lessons with the teacher and showing how computing can be fun.

Computing at School

This organisation promotes computing in school. I lead community meetings which allow teachers of computing to network and share ideas.

Code First Girls

I got involved in Code First Girls because I want to give more girls the opportunity to learn to code.
